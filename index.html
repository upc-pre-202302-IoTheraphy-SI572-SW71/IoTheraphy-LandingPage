    This revolutionary device is designed to take physical therapy to new heights by providing accurate, real-time information about your patients' muscle activity during their therapeutic exercises.

    The IoTherapy Band uses advanced sensors to capture muscle electrical signals, providing detailed data on muscle activity during therapies.
